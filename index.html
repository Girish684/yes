About Jaipur

Jaipur is the capital city of Rajasthan, India. Founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, it is popularly known as the "Pink City" because of the distinct color of its buildings. Jaipur is famous for its rich history, magnificent forts, royal palaces, and colorful bazaars.

Famous Places in Jaipur

Hawa Mahal

Hawa Mahal, also known as the Palace of Winds, is one of Jaipur’s most iconic landmarks. Built in 1799, it features 953 small windows designed to allow royal ladies to observe street festivals.

Amber Fort

Amber Fort is a majestic fort located on a hilltop near Jaipur. It is known for its artistic Hindu-style architecture and beautiful courtyards.

City Palace

The City Palace is a grand palace complex that blends Mughal and Rajasthani architecture. It remains a major tourist attraction and residence of the royal family.

Culture & Festivals

Jaipur is famous for its vibrant culture, traditional dance, music, handicrafts, and delicious Rajasthani cuisine. Festivals like Teej, Gangaur, and the Jaipur Literature Festival attract visitors from around the world.
